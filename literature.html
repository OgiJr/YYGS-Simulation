Atypical Dreams

We desire them while awake
We relive them while asleep
An interpretation or a complication?
Only our will decides
A reality or merely a dream

How are they unique?
Many are prepared to tell you
That the bigger they are
Or the stranger it is
Makes a dream unique

But the fact is
It is we, the dreamers
Who provides the uniqueness
To our desires, our wishes
And give them life

To make a dream unique
One must dream with a sense of selfessness
To do it right by ourselves and also by others
A dash of ingenuity, a pinch of desire
With a whole lot of determination

To make a dream unique
One must dream with a sense of selfishness
To do right by ourselves and also by others
A dash of ingenuity, a pinch of desire
With a whole lot of determination

Many dreams will come and go
Some fantasies, some truth
By only those will endure
Which carry both our passions and our gift

'Tis important to keep in mind
To always keep dreaming and remember
That we must be pushed by our dreams and not our problems.

- Nila Manesh
